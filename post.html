Humans, with one of the highest brain-to-body size and mass ratios of any animal in the world, an incredibly complex structure with more than 100 billion neurons interacting. Continuity with each other every second creates a turning point of difference: Thinking. A saying that later became a fundamental element in Western philosophy by philosopher René Descartes: "Je pense, donc je suis: "Je pense, donc je suis" " - "I think therefore I exist" has contributed to affirming the essential importance of thinking. Humanity is perhaps a "strange" creature with the ability to constantly create, bold thoughts, inventions that transcend time and sensitive adaptation to all changes in life have formed. one of the most living individuals on the planet, and that is the outward manifestation of the continuous thought process.

Why is it said that perception affects thinking? To be honest, this is a two-way interactive process. If thinking is a big building, then perception will be the solid foundation and decide the whole structure and scale of that building. In order to be able to think about a certain problem, thing or phenomenon, we must first and at least have background knowledge about the problem to be considered, which is the stage of perception, searching for angles. In general, it is the most important material as well as the basis of thinking. Thinking as a resolution, dissection to find in the picture to perceive deeper depths, to better understand the individual hidden corners in the common perception, to be able to penetrate the so-called essence that at surface perception can't do it, but humans are always more ambitious!

They return to awareness when thinking is full, they rely on existing solid foundations from which to create new cognitive and thinking domains to be able to conquer... And so on, this creating a development cycle: awareness -> thinking -> higher level awareness. But is awareness and thinking alone enough? Thinking creates a way of thinking, thinking creates a subconscious mind and subconscious mind is all that a person can show on the outside, but have you ever wondered if your way of thinking is appropriate? Before that, your perception was really relevant and deeper, how were you oriented from your education?

Thinking, I'm not sure it gives you a lot of money, but I'm sure thinking gives you the opportunity to be a unique and vibrant individual among over seven billion people on this round earth.
